Question

    G.09 - How to quickly evaluate the capabilities of the UNILIB library ?

Answer

    The UNILIB library tool is a collection of FORTRAN modules that usually can only be linked and executed by the use of a FORTRAN main programme (see the examples) or by the use of IDL/PV-WAVE external calls (see the FAQ T.05).
